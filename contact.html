About Us

Incepted in 2014, Dasen is one of the leading manufacturers, traders and suppliers of textile auxiliaries in China. Having cultivated decades‑long expertise within the textile chemical sector, Dasen has strategically diversified its operations into the battery materials and new‑energy chemicals sectors.

We seek to build a sustainable and secure supply chain for critical battery raw materials. These include:

  • Nickel concentrate, nickel matte, nickel/cobalt mixed hydroxide precipitate (MHP) and nickel sulphate
  • Cobalt ores and concentrates, cobalt hydroxide, cobalt sulphate
  • Primary materials containing lithium, graphite and cobalt
  • Other concentrates, intermediate products and by‑products containing cobalt, nickel and lithium

We transact these critical battery raw materials, delivering consistent feedstock to global smelters. As a principal, we source materials for processing at partner facilities. We identify strategic value‑optimization opportunities for miners and metal‑bearing producers to maximize returns from their mineral outputs.

We cooperate with independent third‑party representatives to implement standard weighing, sampling and moisture‑testing procedures for risk mitigation, which has earned us widespread recognition and trust from global clients.

We are continuously advancing under the expert guidance of our experienced management team. Backed by a well‑trained team of professionals, we strengthen our core competence to reliably fulfil orders for critical battery raw materials.
